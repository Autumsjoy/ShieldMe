How It Works

One AI. Every
platform. Always.

01

Behavioral Fingerprinting

Our AI analyses writing style, emoji patterns, punctuation habits and message timing — not just usernames. The same person behind a fake account gets identified in seconds.

02

Zero-Knowledge Evidence Vault

Every contact attempt is automatically archived in a tamper-proof vault. Not even ShieldMe can alter these records. They meet the legal standard for unaltered evidence under POSH Act and BNS §351.

03

90-Second FIR Generation

Evidence is auto-mapped to cybercrime.gov.in fields. What used to take four days of painful re-explanation now takes 90 seconds. One tap, all platforms reported simultaneously.

04

Real-Time Cross-Platform Watch

One block triggers a watchlist across all platforms. If he surfaces on any channel — new name, new account, different app — you're alerted instantly. Your trusted contacts too.

05

Privacy-Preserving Edge AI

All sensitive scanning runs locally on your device via AMD Ryzen AI NPU. No raw messages ever leave your phone. Your privacy stays yours — always.

06

Escalation Prediction

Our ML model scores threat levels in real time — trained on patterns of how digital harassment escalates to physical danger. It gives you advance warning, not reactive alerts.

Legal Framework

Built around
Indian law.

POSH Act 2013

Internal Compliance for Employers

  • Companies with 10+ employees must have an Internal Committee — ShieldMe gives HR a live case-management dashboard
  • Zero-Knowledge Vault logs cannot be edited by victim or accused, meeting the "unaltered evidence" legal standard
  • Anonymised risk scores protect victim privacy until a formal complaint is triggered
  • Auto-reminders ensure IC responds within the mandatory 90-day POSH deadline
BNS Section 351

Stalking & Cyber Harassment

  • BNS §351 requires proof of "repeated contact despite clear disinterest" — ShieldMe documents every attempt across 4.2+ platforms
  • Cross-platform trail is the gold standard for a §351 conviction — our evidence package covers all channels
  • Evidence auto-mapped to cybercrime.gov.in form fields — 90 seconds vs. a 4-day ordeal
  • IT Act 2000 §66E/67 image-based harassment covered via metadata archiving, without storing the image itself
